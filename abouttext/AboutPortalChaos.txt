Portal Chaos - June 2016
Developed in: Unreal 4.11
Development time: 3 months
Team size: 3
Role: Programmer and Designer

Portal Chaos is a game designed for 2 players to work in conjuction with one another to solve logic puzzles on the scientific equipment in front of them. Only once the correct solutions have been found based on the game's instructional documentation will the portal be stable enough to rescue your fellow scientist on trapped on the other side.

Doublooncay - 2016
Developed in: Unity3D 5.2
Development time: 6 weeks
Team size: 5
Role: Lead programming and Tech

Doubloonacy is a 2D hack-and-slash role playing game where you assume the role of the infamous Gingerbeard. Gingerbeard is on a mission to reclaim his lost treasure while avoiding the dead pirates that came before him and defeat his arch nemesis: Captain Northington.

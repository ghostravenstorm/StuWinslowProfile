Doubloonacy - April 2016
Developed in: Unity3D 5.2.1
Development time: 6 weeks
Team size: 5
Role: Lead programming and techincal director

Doubloonacy is a 2D hack-and-slash role playing game where you assume the role of the infamous Gingerbeard, who is on a mission to reclaim his lost treasure while avoiding the dead pirates that came before him and defeat his arch nemesis: Captain Northington.

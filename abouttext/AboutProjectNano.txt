Project Nano - November 2015
Developed in: Unity3D 5.2.1
Development time: 6 weeks
Team size: 6
Role: Designer and second-hand programmer

Project.nano is a 2D "hacking" game that features a mechanic which the user must use text-based commands to manipulate the environment and solve puzzles. The player must be able to act quickly as slow typing skills will get you caught by the automatron secruity system you are trying to disable.

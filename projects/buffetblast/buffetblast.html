// Buffet Blast ------------------------------

Buffet Blast - Summer 2017 (Student Project) Developed in: Unity3D 5.6.1f1 Development time: 7 weeks Team size: 6 Role: Sr Programmer Summary Couch co-op free-for-all arcade game where 4 players must grab as much food as possible from the buffet counter and eat to explode before anyone else can! Technical Features > Data structure of food types I developed to be designer friendly in the Unity's Inspector that integrates well with how the food spawner systems work. > Implements use of my Nano Timers API.
